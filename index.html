Biography

Hello! I'm Samkeliso Dube, a 32-year-old technology enthusiast from Zimbabwe. Currently, I’m diving deep into the Cloud Engineering track at Alt School, driven by my passion for IT and cloud computing. My journey through Specis College and the National University of Science and Technology has solidified my foundation in Networking and Data Management, fueling my excitement to master cloud technologies. I’m captivated by the transformative power of cloud computing and its ability to revolutionize businesses.

I am fascinated by the potential of cloud computing to transform businesses and streamline operations.
